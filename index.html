About Us

Welcome To Shikhar Adventure!

Shikhar Adventure is a local-based trekking agency in Nepal, owned and operated by Dambar Thapa, a seasoned trekking guide with over two decades of experience in the industry. With a team of experienced and knowledgeable guides, Shikhar Adventure offers a wide range of trekking packages to suit every level of trekker, from beginners to experienced adventurers.

We are dedicated to giving our clients the best trekking experience in Nepal. We specialize in organizing trekking tours to some of the most beautiful locations in the Himalayas, such as the Everest Base Camp, the Annapurna Circuit, and the Langtang Valley, among others. With a focus on safety, comfort, and adventure, our trekking packages are designed to provide a one-of-a-kind and unforgettable experience.

" Your Trail Companion in the Himalayas "

To summarize, Shikhar Adventure offers an exceptional trekking experience in Nepal. Our commitment to sustainable tourism, seasoned guides, and personalized services ensures a safe, comfortable, and unforgettable Himalayan adventure. Reach out to us today to secure your trekking tour!
